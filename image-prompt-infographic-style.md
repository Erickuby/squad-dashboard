# Infographic-Style Image Prompt for "AI Intensifies Work" Post

## Reference Style
Clean, professional infographic with 4 key components, each with simple icons, navy color palette, structured layout.

## Detailed Infographic Prompt

**For DALL-E 3 / Midjourney:**

```
A clean, professional infographic titled "AI ACCELERATES WORK" in bold modern typography at the top. Four key research findings displayed in a 2x2 grid layout, each with a simple icon and brief text:

1. Top Left - Icon: Speedometer or clock with arrows pointing clockwise showing fast rotation. Text: "Work Pace ↑ 2-3x faster"

2. Top Right - Icon: Expanding circle or branching tree diagram showing outward growth. Text: "Task Scope ↑ Broader range"

3. Bottom Left - Icon: Stacked document or output pile growing larger. Text: "Output Volume ↑ More completed"

4. Bottom Right - Icon: Target or bullseye with hit marks, or upward arrow trajectory. Text: "Impact ↑ Higher quality results"

Color palette: Navy blue background (#1a237e or similar), white text, cyan/electric blue accent highlights for the arrows and icons. Clean, flat design style similar to modern business infographics. Subtle grid or geometric pattern in background for professional feel. Icons should be simple, minimalist line art or solid shapes. Overall composition balanced and symmetrical. Professional, LinkedIn-appropriate aesthetic. High contrast, easy to read. No clutter or overwhelming details. Style: clean corporate infographic, similar to Canva business templates.
```

**Alternative Prompt (More Detailed):**

```
Professional infographic on navy blue background featuring "AI ACCELERATES WORK" as headline. Four quadrants with white boxes or cards, each containing:

Box 1: Simple speedometer icon with needle in red zone. Text: "Pace: 2-3x Faster"

Box 2: Expanding diagram icon (like ripples in water or branching lines). Text: "Scope: Wider Range"

Box 3: Upward trending bar chart or stacked documents. Text: "Volume: More Output"

Box 4: Arrow pointing upward to target or star. Text: "Impact: Better Results"

Design: Clean cards with subtle shadows, white backgrounds against navy, cyan accent lines connecting elements. Typography: Sans-serif, bold headings, clear body text. Icons: Simple line art or flat icons in navy and cyan. Overall: Balanced, symmetrical, professional business infographic style. 4K quality, sharp edges, vector-style aesthetic. Similar to corporate presentation graphics or business school diagrams.
```

**Midjourney-specific parameters:**
```
--style raw --ar 4:3 --v 6 --q 2
```

**Key Design Elements to Emphasize:**
- Navy blue background (professional, trustworthy)
- White text (high contrast, readable)
- Cyan/electric blue accents (modern tech feel)
- Simple, clean icons (not cartoonish)
- 2x2 grid layout (balanced)
- Brief, punchy text (scannable)
- Professional infographic style (LinkedIn-appropriate)
- No clutter or visual noise

**Recommended Format:** 1200 x 900 or 1600 x 1200 (infographic aspect ratio, can crop to 1200x627 for LinkedIn post)

## Why This Works for LinkedIn

- **Scannable:** 4 quick points, easy to digest
- **Research-backed:** Cites actual findings
- **Visual hierarchy:** Clear structure, easy to follow
- **Professional:** Matches LinkedIn's business aesthetic
- **Shareable:** Infographics get saved and shared more
- **Complements post:** Visualizes the key data points from the post
