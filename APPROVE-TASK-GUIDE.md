# How to Approve YouTube Script Task

**Good news!** The Squad Dashboard now has approve/reject buttons directly on task cards. No need to open a modal.

## Quick Steps

### 1. Open Squad Dashboard
Go to your Squad Dashboard (localhost:3000) and switch to **Task Board** view.

### 2. Find Your Task
Look in the **"Waiting Approval"** column. You'll see your YouTube script task.

### 3. Review the Work
**Click on the task card** to open the full task detail modal. There you'll see:

- **Full script content** (1,780 words, 10 minutes)
- **All comments** from the Copywriter
- **Chat history** of the work
- **Add comments** if you want to provide feedback

## Troubleshooting

**Buttons not showing?**
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
- Wait 30 seconds for auto-refresh
- Check you're on "Task Board" view (not "Squad" view)

**Can't see script?**
- Click the task card (don't hover)
- Scroll down in the modal
- Look for comments from "Copywriter (agent)"

**Notion not syncing?**
- Check browser console for errors
- Make sure Notion integration is configured
- Try refreshing and approving again
